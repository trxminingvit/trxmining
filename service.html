Frequently Asked Questions

What is TRX mining?

TRX mining is the process of validating transactions on the TRON blockchain and earning TRX coins as rewards. Our platform provides an easy way to participate in TRX mining without technical knowledge.

How do I start mining?

To start mining, navigate to the Quantify section, select your preferred mining plan, and click on 'Start Quantification'. You can also invest in AI robot contracts in the Invest section for higher returns.

How do withdrawals work?

You can withdraw your earnings by going to the profile section and clicking on 'Withdraw'. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and your TRX wallet address. Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
